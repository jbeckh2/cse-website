About CSE

Founded in 2009, CSE has evolved from hands-on project work into a consulting firm focused on solving complex problems and supporting meaningful projects.

With a background in specialized construction and Department of Defense contracting, CSE understands how projects are built and executed. That experience, combined with advanced academic training including a PhD, provides a unique ability to understand both operational challenges and broader systems.

Today, CSE works with businesses, government entities, and organizations to identify opportunities, navigate challenges, and develop practical solutions that lead to results.
